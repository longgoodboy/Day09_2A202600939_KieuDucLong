# P1 SLA and Escalation SOP

- P1 incidents must be announced first in the incident Slack channel `#ops-p1` and the on-call phone bridge.
- The first customer-impact notification must be sent within 5 minutes after P1 confirmation.
- If the primary on-call does not acknowledge within 10 minutes, escalate to the Incident Commander and Support Manager.
- P1 ticket updates must continue every 15 minutes until mitigation is confirmed.
- Sources: P1 SLA and Escalation SOP.
