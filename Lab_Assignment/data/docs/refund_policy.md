# Refund and Return Policy

- Standard refunds are allowed within 7 days for eligible physical products.
- Flash Sale items normally are not refundable, except when a manufacturer defect is verified within 7 days.
- Digital products are not refunded after activation, except duplicate charge, failed delivery, or verified technical defect.
- Store credit for approved non-cash compensation is 80% of the original paid amount.
- For policy version conflicts, use the policy active on the purchase date.
- Sources: Refund and Return Policy.
